Welcome to Raffles FinTech Limited

Driven by the proprietary AIDA framework

Artificial Intelligence & Data Analytics at the core of every funding solution we engineer.

Raffles Fintech Limited "RFT" is an integrated, multi-disciplinary supply chain trade finance funding solutions provider headquartered in Hong Kong. Driven by the proprietary "AIDA" (Artificial Intelligence & Data Analytics) framework, RFT seamlessly bridges the gap between traditional fund raising with strategic procurement funding, accounts receivables advances, revolving credits, forfaiting of trade instruments, bankers acceptance, convertible bonds, SAFE loans and tokenisation of real world assets to fund capital expenditures.

Through three strategic business units (SBUs), RFT engineers cost-effective, future-ready, and compliant supply chain trade finance integrated solutions — including capital expenditure funding through asset-backed tokenisation. RFT empowers fast growing emerging companies across the ASEAN region and China with funds to fulfill sales orders, increasing revenues, gross margins, and eventually market capitalization when they IPO.
