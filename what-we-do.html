What We Do

Three strategic business units engineering integrated supply chain trade finance solutions, powered by the AIDA framework.

1

AIDA Revolving Credit

AI and data-analytics driven revolving credit, accounts receivables factoring and cost-effective procurement funding — helping fast-growing companies convert sales orders into working capital without significant dilution.

2

Trade Finance Instrument Advisory & Arrangement

Facility analysis, structuring advisory, arrangement of bankers acceptances, forfaiting of trade instruments, and "guarantor of last resort" bank guarantee assurance with our panel of licensed institutional partners.

3

Asset-Backed Funding

Tokenisation of capital intensive assets and REIT-like structures, alongside convertible instruments and SAFE loans, to maximise capital efficiency for capital expenditure.

How It Works

An integrated, turnkey process

RFT collaborates with licensed financial institutions specialising in trade finance revolving credit to deliver end-to-end funding solutions.

1. Assess

Our AIDA framework analyses your sales orders, receivables and supply chain data to determine the optimal funding structure.

2. Structure

We arrange the right mix of revolving credit, receivables advances, guarantees or tokenised instruments for your growth stage.

3. Fund

Capital is deployed through our panel of licensed institutional funding partners, matched to your working capital cycle.

4. Scale

Fulfil more sales orders, grow revenue and margins, and build towards a stronger market capitalization at IPO.
